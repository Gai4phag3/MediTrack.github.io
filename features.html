Features of Our Automatic Pill Dispenser

What is our Solution?

Our project is focused on designing and building an automatic pill dispenser to assist individuals who have difficulty remembering to take their medications. The system consists of a pill dispenser that is programmed to release the correct medication at a specified time, ensuring that the user receives the proper dose without needing to manually manage their pills. In addition to the dispenser, we have incorporated an alarm feature that activates at the scheduled time, alerting the user to take their medication. The device also includes a screen that displays a clear reminder message, reinforcing the alarm and providing visual confirmation of the task. The goal of this project is to improve medication adherence, particularly for individuals with cognitive impairments, busy schedules, or elderly users, by offering an easy-to-use, reliable solution that supports their health management and promotes greater independence.
